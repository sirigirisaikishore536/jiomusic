Pichai Sundararajan (born June 10, 1972 ), also known as Sundar Pichai ( / ˈsʊndɑːr pɪˈtʃaɪ / ), is an Indian American business executive, the chief executive officer (CEO) of Alphabet Inc. and its subsidiary Google LLC. Pichai began his career as a materials engineer and joined Google as a management executive in 2004.
